FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does A1 Technovation work with international clients?
A1 Technovation works with clients across 20+ countries, including the US, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Australia. All services are delivered remotely with full reporting transparency.
Does SEO take a long time to show results?
Most clients see measurable ranking improvements within 3 to 6 months. The timeline depends on current domain authority, competition level, and execution intensity.
What is the difference between SEO, AEO, and GEO?
SEO targets traditional rankings. AEO targets snippets, People Also Ask, and voice search. GEO targets AI-generated answers in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
Does A1 Technovation use white-hat SEO only?
Yes. All work is white-hat. No link schemes, cloaking, or doorway pages. Clients own the content, links, and configurations after the engagement.
Can a small business afford A1 Technovation's services?
Yes. Scope and investment are matched to the client's current size and target market. A free audit is the best first step.
Does the agency provide monthly reporting?
All clients receive monthly performance reports covering organic sessions, keyword movement, lead volume, and technical health.
Do clients own the content and links after the engagement?
Yes. All deliverables belong to the client. There are no proprietary tools the client loses access to when the engagement ends.
Is A1 Technovation the right fit for ecommerce brands?
Yes. We run full ecommerce SEO programs for Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento stores, including product optimization, category architecture, schema, crawl fixes, and buyer-intent content.
