Video Funnel Strategy

Build Trust Before Buyers Search Again

YouTube is useful because it sits between awareness, education, search, and retargeting. We use video campaigns to explain your offer, answer objections, demonstrate value, and bring warmer audiences back into Google Search, landing pages, or lead funnels.

  • Top-of-funnel video campaigns introduce the problem, category, and brand promise
  • Mid-funnel campaigns educate viewers with demos, case studies, comparisons, and proof
  • Retargeting campaigns reach site visitors, video viewers, and channel engagers with stronger offers
  • Measurement includes direct conversions, assisted conversions, engaged views, and remarketing value

FAQ

YouTube PPC Questions Answered

Do YouTube ads generate direct leads?
They can, especially with remarketing and strong offers. YouTube also often supports assisted conversions by warming buyers before they return through search, direct traffic, or retargeting.
How long should YouTube ads be?
There is no single perfect length. We plan the video around the job: Shorts need speed, bumper ads need one idea, and in-stream ads can run longer when they educate, prove, and keep attention.
Can YouTube work with Google Search campaigns?
Yes. YouTube can build awareness and retargeting pools, while Search captures high-intent demand. Together they often create a stronger Google Ads funnel than Search alone.
