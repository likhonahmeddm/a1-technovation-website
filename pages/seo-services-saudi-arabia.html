Arabic SEO

Arabic and English SEO for Saudi Search Behavior

Saudi SEO works best when Arabic and English search are treated as separate strategic systems that share technical foundations.

Bilingual Delivery

Translation Is Not Arabic SEO

Arabic keyword research must consider root forms, Saudi phrasing, commercial search intent, right-to-left formatting and how Arabic SERPs differ from English SERPs. English SEO matters too, especially for international investors, expats, B2B buyers and regional partners.

  • Arabic and English keyword maps built independently, not copied from one language to another
  • Hreflang and canonical strategy for bilingual or multilingual site structures
  • Arabic metadata, headings, body copy, FAQs and internal links aligned with Saudi search intent
  • Structured data that keeps entity signals consistent across language variants
