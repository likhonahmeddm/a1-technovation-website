FAQ

X/Twitter PPC Questions Answered

Is X/Twitter good for direct-response ads?
It can be, but it usually works best when direct-response campaigns are supported by strong topical relevance, retargeting, and authority-building content. Cold conversion campaigns need careful testing.
What businesses should advertise on X?
X is strongest for SaaS, tech, media, finance, creator-led brands, events, and businesses where opinion, speed, public dialogue, or industry conversation influence buyers.
Can X campaigns support LinkedIn or YouTube campaigns?
Yes. X can amplify fast-moving ideas and build retargeting pools, while LinkedIn reaches decision-makers and YouTube provides deeper education. Together they can support a stronger B2B funnel.
