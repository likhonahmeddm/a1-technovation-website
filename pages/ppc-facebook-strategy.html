Facebook Funnel Strategy

From Cold Reach to Qualified Enquiries

Facebook works best when campaigns are built as a funnel, not as one broad ad set aimed at everyone. We separate prospecting, engagement, retargeting, and conversion campaigns so each stage has the right creative, audience, budget, and success metric.

  • Cold campaigns introduce the offer with clear visual hooks and simple value propositions
  • Warm campaigns retarget page engagers, video viewers, website visitors, and abandoned carts
  • Conversion campaigns optimize for leads, purchases, bookings, calls, or qualified form submissions
  • Creative fatigue is monitored so winning ads are refreshed before performance drops

FAQ

Facebook PPC Questions Answered

How much budget do Facebook ads need?
The right budget depends on your market, offer, and conversion value. For most lead campaigns, we recommend enough spend to generate consistent weekly conversion data rather than tiny tests that never leave the learning stage.
Do Facebook ads work for B2B?
Yes, but the strategy is different. Facebook is often useful for retargeting, founder-led content, webinar promotion, and lower-friction B2B offers, while LinkedIn may be stronger for direct decision-maker targeting.
Can you manage Facebook and Instagram together?
Yes. Both run through Meta Ads Manager, but we still review placement performance separately so Instagram and Facebook creative can be optimized by behavior, not lumped together blindly.
