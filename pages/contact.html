Common Questions

Quick Answers

How quickly will I see results from digital marketing?
It depends on the service. PPC campaigns can generate leads within days. SEO typically shows significant results in 3-6 months. Social media growth is steady from month one. We set realistic expectations from the start and share monthly progress reports.
What's the minimum budget to work with A1 Technovation?
Our monthly retainers now start from $200 for focused social media support and from $300 for PPC management. Web design projects start from $500 one-time, while broader growth engagements typically range from $650 to $4,500+ depending on scope and deliverables.
Do you work with businesses outside Bangladesh?
Absolutely. We have active clients in the UK, USA, Canada, and Australia. Our team works across time zones and is experienced with international markets and platforms.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
No long-term lock-ins. We work on month-to-month retainers after an initial 3-month foundation period. We prefer earning your continued business through results rather than contracts.
How do you measure and report on results?
Every client gets a custom reporting dashboard and a monthly performance review call. We track KPIs agreed upfront - traffic, leads, conversions, revenue - so you always know exactly what's happening with your investment.
