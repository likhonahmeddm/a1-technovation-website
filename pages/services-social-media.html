Social Media FAQs

Common questions before starting social media support

Do you create the content strategy or only post designs?

We focus on the broader system as well, including content pillars, messaging direction, campaign ideas, and platform-specific communication strategy.

Can you manage multiple social platforms?

Yes. We can support one or several platforms depending on where your audience is most active and which channels matter most to the brand.

Is paid advertising included?

Paid social can be included as part of a broader campaign plan when boosting reach or targeting a specific audience is important.

Will this help the website too?

Yes. Better social communication often supports website traffic, trust, and offer awareness, especially when messaging is aligned with your key service pages.
