FAQ

Social Media Questions Answered

Which social platforms should my business focus on?
It depends on your audience and industry. B2C brands typically see best results on Instagram and Facebook. B2B companies often find LinkedIn most valuable. We research where your specific audience spends time and recommend a platform strategy accordingly.
Do I need to approve content before it's posted?
Yes - we prepare a monthly content calendar with all posts for your review and approval before scheduling. You have full visibility and control over what gets published, while we handle all the creation and scheduling work.
How do you measure social media ROI?
We track follower growth, engagement rates, reach, website traffic from social, and most importantly - leads and revenue attributable to social channels. Monthly reports clearly show the business impact, not just vanity metrics.
Can you manage social media alongside our existing marketing?
Absolutely. We integrate seamlessly with your existing marketing team and channels. We'll align social content with your campaigns, promotions, and brand messaging for a coherent cross-channel presence.
