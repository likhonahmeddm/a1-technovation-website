The Problem

Three Gaps Costing HVAC Companies Calls Right Now

In most mid-to-large US cities, 15 to 40 HVAC contractors compete for the same search spots. The top Local Pack positions collect the calls most companies never see.

Gap 1: No Local Pack Presence

The Google Local Pack dominates HVAC search pages. Without an optimized Google Business Profile, consistent citations, and strong reviews, your company competes for a much smaller slice of traffic.

Gap 2: No Emergency Search Coverage

Searches like AC not working, emergency furnace repair, and heat not turning on are among the highest-converting HVAC queries. Most sites have no pages built for them.

Gap 3: No Seasonal Content Strategy

AC queries spike from April through August. Heating queries spike from September through February. A reactive content plan misses the most profitable months.

Residential vs Commercial

Commercial HVAC SEO and Residential HVAC SEO Need Different Tracks

Residential HVAC SEO targets homeowners with urgent local-intent searches. The buying cycle is short for emergency repairs, seasonal for maintenance, and mid-length for planned replacements.

Commercial HVAC SEO targets facilities managers, property managers, building owners, and procurement teams. Those searches are research-heavy and need pages for offices, retail, industrial, multifamily, healthcare, and hospitality.

  • Residential focus: Google Local Pack, reviews, proximity, emergency repair, and seasonal replacement demand
  • Commercial focus: preventive maintenance contracts, rooftop units, chillers, ASHRAE standards, SEER2 compliance, and case studies
  • Separate content tracks for contractors serving both residential and commercial markets

Our small business SEO services cover HVAC businesses at every scale, from single-location residential contractors to multi-market commercial operations.

FAQ

Frequently Asked HVAC SEO Questions

Does SEO work for HVAC companies?
Yes. HVAC companies that rank in the Google Local Pack and organic results for service queries generate consistent inbound call volume with no cost per click. A page that ranks today can keep generating leads for years.
Does HVAC SEO take a long time to show results?
Most HVAC companies see measurable Local Pack movement within 60 to 90 days. Organic ranking improvements usually appear in months 3 to 5, with larger lead growth often developing in months 6 to 9.
What HVAC keywords matter most?
Emergency service queries, local installation queries, and local repair queries are the highest-value categories. Maintenance keywords are lower volume but can convert strongly when searchers are ready to schedule.
Do I need a separate page for every city I serve?
Yes. Each city needs a dedicated page with unique content, local signals, and its own keyword targeting. A single homepage cannot rank effectively across every city in a service area.
Can you help commercial HVAC companies rank for B2B queries?
Yes. Commercial HVAC SEO requires specification-level content, segment-specific service pages, case studies, and B2B authority signals for facilities managers, property managers, and procurement teams.
Do online reviews affect HVAC SEO rankings?
Google reviews influence Local Pack rankings. Review volume, recency, and rating quality help Google understand prominence and trust, especially in competitive HVAC markets.
