FAQ

PPC Questions Answered

How quickly will I see results from PPC?
Unlike SEO, PPC delivers results almost immediately. We typically launch campaigns within 5–7 business days and you'll see initial data within the first week. Meaningful optimisation and ROI improvement happens over the first 30–60 days as we gather performance data.
What's the minimum ad spend you recommend?
We recommend a minimum of $500/month in ad spend to gather sufficient data for meaningful optimisation. Below this, campaigns struggle to collect enough conversion data to improve. For competitive industries, $1,500+ delivers significantly better results.
How do you measure and report campaign ROI?
We set up complete conversion tracking — phone calls, form submissions, purchases — so every campaign is tied to actual business outcomes. Monthly reports show spend, clicks, conversions, cost per conversion, and ROAS. You always know your exact ROI.
Can you take over campaigns I'm currently running?
Absolutely. We conduct a thorough audit of existing campaigns, identify wasted spend and missed opportunities, and either restructure or rebuild them based on what we find. Most clients see significant efficiency improvements within 60 days of a takeover.
