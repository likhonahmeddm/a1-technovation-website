Multilingual SEO

Arabic and English Together

The UAE search market splits across two languages with different user behaviors, keyword landscapes, and SERP features. A1 Technovation builds bilingual SEO architecture from day one.

Separate URL Structures

English URLs and Arabic `/ar/` URLs are planned as paired pages with correct `hreflang="en-ae"` and `hreflang="ar-ae"` signals.

Independent Keyword Research

Arabic SEO content is written around Arabic search behavior, not translated English keyword lists.

Unified Entity Signals

Schema declares both language versions as part of the same Organization entity while allowing each page to rank and be cited independently.
