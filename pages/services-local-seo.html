FAQ

Local SEO Questions Answered

Common questions from business owners considering local SEO for the first time - answered honestly.

How long does local SEO take to show results?
Most local SEO campaigns start showing measurable movement within 6 to 12 weeks, especially in Google Business visibility, map pack reach, and location-based keywords. More competitive markets can take longer, but momentum builds quickly when the foundation is right.
Can you help businesses with multiple locations?
Yes. We build location-page structures, optimise each Google Business Profile independently, and standardise citations so every branch has a better chance to rank in its own service area without cannibalising the others.
Do I need a Google Business Profile for local SEO?
In most cases, yes. Your Google Business Profile is one of the most important assets for ranking in maps and local packs. We optimise it alongside your website, reviews, and citation profile to maximise local visibility across all three pillars of local search ranking.
What kinds of businesses benefit most from local SEO?
Local SEO works especially well for law firms, clinics, contractors, salons, repair shops, restaurants, plumbers, landscapers, and any business that serves customers in a defined geographic area - whether from a fixed location or a service radius.
What is the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?
Local SEO focuses on ranking your business for geographically specific searches - like "dentist near me" or "plumber in London" - and in Google Maps results. Regular SEO targets national or global keyword visibility. Local SEO prioritises your Google Business Profile, map pack position, citation consistency, and location-specific landing pages rather than broad content authority alone.
How do you measure local SEO success?
We track local keyword rankings, Google Business Profile performance (calls, direction requests, website clicks), map pack impressions, citation consistency scores, and review velocity. Every metric we report is tied to real leads and business outcomes - not vanity numbers. You will always know exactly what your investment is producing.
