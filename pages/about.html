Our Founder

Built by a Marketer Who's Been in the Trenches

Hi, I'm Likhon Ahmed — founder and CEO of A1 Technovation. I started this agency after years of watching businesses waste money on digital marketing without any real strategy or accountability.

I believed there had to be a better way — built on data, transparency, and a genuine commitment to client outcomes. So in 2019, A1 Technovation was born with a simple promise: deliver measurable results or don't charge for them.

Today, our team of 15+ specialists has helped over 200 businesses across Bangladesh, the UK, and the USA achieve extraordinary online growth. We're proud of every result — and we're just getting started.
