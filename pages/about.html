About A1 Technovation

Dhaka-Based SEO and Digital Marketing Team Built for Measurable Growth

A1 Technovation is a digital marketing agency in Dhaka, Bangladesh helping businesses rank on Google, get cited by AI engines, launch faster websites, and turn search visibility into leads, sales, and long-term brand authority.

7+
Years Building Search Growth
Founder-Led Strategy

Built by Likhon Ahmed for Search, AI Visibility, and Revenue

A1 Technovation is led by Likhon Ahmed, founder and CEO of the agency. Likhon built A1 around a simple idea: digital marketing should be measurable, transparent, and tied to business outcomes instead of vague activity reports.

Since 2018, the agency has grown from a focused SEO practice in Dhaka into a multi-disciplinary growth team covering SEO services, AEO optimization, GEO optimization, web design, PPC advertising, social media, and ecommerce SEO.

Today, our team of 15+ specialists supports businesses in Bangladesh and international markets with technical execution, strategic thinking, and a commitment to long-term organic authority.
